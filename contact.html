FAQs

What happens if I'm not satisfied with the final design or illustration?

Answer: Your satisfaction is the top priority. In the rare event that you're not completely satisfied with the final product, I offer a revision process to address any concerns or changes you may have. I will work closely with you to make adjustments until we achieve a design or illustration that meets your expectations.

What measures do you have in place if there are errors or issues with the final deliverables?

Answer: While I strive for perfection in every project, I understand that mistakes can occasionally occur. If there are errors or issues with the final deliverables, I am committed to resolving them promptly and professionally. Whether it's a typographical error in a branding asset or a technical glitch in an illustration file, I will take full responsibility and work to rectify the situation to your satisfaction.

What is your refund policy in case of unforeseen circumstances or dissatisfaction with the final product?

Answer: I stand behind the quality of work I put out and aim to provide a positive experience for every client. If, for any reason, you're not satisfied with the final product or if unforeseen circumstances arise, I am open to discussing potential solutions, including refunds or alternative arrangements. My goal is to ensure that you're completely satisfied with the outcome of your project, and I will do whatever it takes to make it right.
